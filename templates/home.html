The Science

Built on proven
computer vision theory

Our pipeline is grounded in decades of computer vision research, combined with the latest advances in large language models for semantic understanding.

Convolutional Neural Networks

Custom CNN backbone optimized for texture, color, and shape-based material recognition at 4K resolution.

Transfer Learning

Pre-trained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on 2M+ industrial waste images for domain-specific precision.

Multimodal LLM Fusion

Vision embeddings passed to Gemini Pro for semantic reasoning — bridging perception and language.

TensorRT Optimization

NVIDIA TensorRT quantization reduces model size 4× while maintaining 99.2% precision at inference.
